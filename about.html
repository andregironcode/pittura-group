Pittura is Italian
for painting.

We chose the name because that's how we treat digital work — as craft. Every pixel placed with intent, every line of code a brushstroke.

Our story

Born in Dubai. Built for anywhere.

Pittura Group started as a two-person studio with a simple frustration: too many agencies in the region were selling templates as craft and juniors as seniors. We believed the Gulf deserved product work at a global standard — designed with regional fluency, engineered without shortcuts.

Today we're a multidisciplinary team of designers, engineers and strategists working from Dubai Design District, shipping websites, software and apps for startups, family businesses and enterprises across the UAE, the wider Gulf and beyond.

What hasn't changed: small senior teams, honest timelines, and an obsession with the details most people never notice — but everyone feels.

What we stand for

Our values.

01

Craft is the strategy

Quality compounds. A product that feels exceptional earns trust, referrals and retention no ad budget can buy. We sweat details because details are the product.

02

Radical transparency

Live staging links, weekly demos, honest estimates and bad news delivered early. You'll always know exactly where your project stands — and what it costs.

03

Global standard, regional fluency

We benchmark against the world's best studios while designing for this region — Arabic-first interfaces, RTL done properly, and cultural nuance no offshore team can fake.

04

Partners, not vendors

We win when your product wins. That means telling you when a feature isn't worth building — even when we'd profit from building it.
