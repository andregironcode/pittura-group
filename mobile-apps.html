FAQ

Before you ask.

It depends on your product, not on fashion. Heavy animation, hardware access or platform-specific UX favours native; content and commerce apps often thrive on Flutter or React Native at half the build cost. We'll recommend one and show you exactly why.

A focused MVP typically starts from AED 90k cross-platform or AED 140k native. Complex products with realtime features, payments or marketplaces range upward from there. You'll get a fixed, itemised quote after discovery.

Most MVPs go from kickoff to store approval in 12–16 weeks: 2–3 weeks of design sprints, 8–10 weeks of development in fortnightly demos, then beta testing and submission.
